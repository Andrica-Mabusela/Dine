About The Owner




Dine is owned by top chef Andrica mabusela, who had 10 years of experience prior to opening up this restaurant.

Andrica graduated from a culinary school in paris at the age of 21 and worked with some of the most talented people in the field

To date, Andrica has 25 years of experience working in the restaurant and hospitality industry. He has worked in top class restaurants in paris, New York and hong kong, He brings his skills of diverse food to our shores.
